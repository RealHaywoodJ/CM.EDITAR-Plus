## Responsibilities

- **Project owner / lead maintainer** sets direction, reviews and merges PRs,
  cuts releases, and is the final authority on contributor disputes.
- **Release manager** tags releases, publishes signed installers, and updates
  the `CHANGELOG.md` on every tag.

## Reaching the maintainers

- Routine questions: open a GitHub Discussion or Issue.
- Security reports: see [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) — do **not** open a public
  issue for vulnerabilities.
- Anything that needs a private channel: <dev@eth-munson.com>.
